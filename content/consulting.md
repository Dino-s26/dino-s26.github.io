# Consulting & Professional Services

Hello! I'm Dino, an Infrastructure SRE with extensive experience in networking, cloud technologies, and automation. I offer consulting services to help you scale, optimize, and secure your infrastructure.

## Services Offered

### 1. Cloud Infrastructure & SRE
- **Infrastructure as Code (IaC):** Implementing Terraform, Ansible, and Pulumi for reproducible environments.
- **Cloud Migration:** Seamlessly moving workloads to AWS, GCP, or Azure.
- **Kubernetes & Containerization:** Setting up and managing K8s clusters (self-hosted or managed).
- **CI/CD Pipelines:** Automating your deployment workflows for better efficiency.

### 2. Network Infrastructure
- **Network Design & Optimization:** Tailored solutions for Cisco, F5, Palo Alto, and Fortinet environments.
- **Security Audits:** Reviewing firewall rules and network security postures.
- **SD-WAN & Hybrid connectivity.**

### 3. Monitoring & Observability
- Setting up Datadog, New Relic, or open-source stacks (Prometheus, Grafana, ELK).
- Implementing proactive alerting and dashboarding.
